where to go, what to paste, who to contact, and what to say. Work top to bottom.

Track progress in the checklist at the bottom.

RAVEN is a borrower verification platform built for community banks and credit unions. RAVEN serves community banks across the Southeast and nationally.

## 1. Tier 1: Free directories (one sitting, ~half a day)

Do these in order. Have the asset kit open in one tab.

### Crunchbase
- Go to: https://www.crunchbase.com → create a personal account → search "RAVEN reportraven" to confirm no profile exists → "Add a Company" (in the profile menu; or https://www.crunchbase.com/add-new)
- Paste: one-liner, 50-word blurb, categories (FinTech, Financial Services, Identity Management), founder, founded year, HQ
- The website link is followed on paid tiers only, but the profile itself ranks for "RAVEN" searches and feeds ZoomInfo/PitchBook-style aggregators

### G2
- Go to: https://sell.g2.com → "Create a Profile" (free tier)
- Category: pick "Identity Verification Software" primary; "Digital Lending Platforms" secondary
- Paste: 150-word blurb, screenshots, competitor alternatives
- After listing: ask 2-3 friendly design partners/pilot users for reviews; G2 profiles with even 2 reviews rank dramatically better on category pages

### Capterra / GetApp / Software Advice (one submission, Gartner Digital Markets)
- Go to: https://www.capterra.com/vendors → "Get Listed" (free basic listing)
- One form covers all three properties
- Category: "Financial Services Software" and "Loan Origination Software"
- Paste: 150-word blurb + screenshots

### ZoomInfo
- Go to: https://www.zoominfo.com/claim-your-business → claim/create the company page
- Mostly auto-populated; correct the description with the 50-word blurb

### PitchBook
- Go to: https://pitchbook.com → footer → "Submit company data" (or email profiles@pitchbook.com)
- Provide: company facts + 50-word blurb

### Built In
- Go to: https://builtin.com → "For Employers" → free company profile
- Paste: 150-word blurb; skip paid employer-branding upsells

### Clutch
- Go to: https://clutch.co/get-listed → free profile
- Category: Fintech / Custom Software; paste 150-word blurb

### GoodFirms
- Go to: https://www.goodfirms.co → "Get Listed" → free
- Same categories and copy as Clutch

### fintechvendors.com
- Go to: https://fintechvendors.com → look for "Add your company"/submit link
- Category: Verification / Lending; paste 50-word blurb

### SaaSHub
- Go to: https://www.saashub.com → "Submit a Service"
- List alternatives: Blend, Truv, Plaid IDV (this is what makes the listing findable)

### AlternativeTo
- Go to: https://alternativeto.net → "Add an application"
- Same alternatives list

### Product Hunt (optional, do last)
- Go to: https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new
- This is a launch event, not a listing; do it on a Tuesday-Thursday, pair with a LinkedIn post
- Copy: one-liner + 50-word blurb + the fraud graphic + demo screenshots

adjust the name. Expect $500-$3,000/yr depending on association. Ask specifically whether
associate membership includes a **directory listing with a website link** — that's the SEO
asset; the conference discounts are gravy.

## 3. Tier 3: Contributed trade articles (free links, reuse our research)

Rule for all of these: strip every RAVEN product mention from the article body. The link
comes from the author identification line, and editors reject anything promotional.

### ABA Banking Journal
- Guidelines: https://bankingjournal.aba.com/about/author-guidelines/
- Format: 500-2,000 words, educational, non-promotional; byline + brief author/company ID
- Pitch: adapt the 1-in-116 article into "What community banks miss about application fraud"
- Submit per the instructions on the guidelines page (editorial contact listed there)

### BAI / ProSight Banking Strategies
- Guidelines: https://www.bai.org/banking-strategies/submission-guidelines/
- Pitch: "The fraud fix and the speed fix are the same fix" (digital-retreat angle)

### Independent Banker (ICBA)
- Go to: https://independentbanker.org → contact editorial
- Pitch: the fictitious-employer VOE attack surface piece; ICBA already writes fraud guides, this extends theirs with mortgage data

### Bank Director
- Go to: https://www.bankdirector.com → "Contribute"/editorial contact
- Pitch: the COO account-opening story — "The bank that turned off online account opening was half right"

### The Financial Brand
- Go to: https://thefinancialbrand.com → "Write for Us"/contributor guidelines
- Pitch: neobank vs. community bank account-opening data (92% abandonment, 44% share)

### MortgageNewsDaily
- Truv earned 51 links here; entry point is data-driven commentary
- Pitch the fraud-by-the-numbers piece to their editorial contact with the chart offered as an embeddable graphic

**Email template — editor pitch**

> Subject: Contributed article: 1 in 116 mortgage applications shows fraud — what it means for community banks
>
> Hi [name],
>
> I run RAVEN, a verification platform for community banks, and I write
> data-driven analysis of community bank lending. I'd like to contribute a
> non-promotional piece built on Cotality's 2025 fraud report and Fannie Mae's
> fraud program data:
>
> - 1 in 116 applications shows material misrepresentation (1 in 106 on purchase)
> - Income misrepresentation leads confirmed findings at 46%
> - Fannie Mae's fictitious-employer watchlist is at 63 entities, some with

>
> Working title: "[publication-appropriate title]". ~1,200 words, original
> analysis, no product mentions. Happy to send the full draft.
>
> Isaac Welch · RAVEN · reportraven.tech

## 4. Tier 4: Press, local, podcasts (ongoing)

### SC business press (pitch the bank deep-dive data as local stories)
- SC Biz News properties: Columbia Regional Business Report, Charleston Regional
  Business Journal, GSA Business Report → https://scbiznews.com (editorial contacts on site)
- Pitch: "New analysis of [Charleston/Columbia/Upstate] community bank performance" using
  the audit-page data; offer the reporter the underlying FDIC/HMDA numbers

### PR wire (only when there's actual news)
- prnewswire.com or businesswire.com, $350-$800 per release
- Triggers: notable bank goes live, original research report, funding

### CBANC
- Vendor participation where community bankers ask peers for recommendations
- Already monitored via /cbanc-monitor; answer relevant threads, keep the profile complete

### Podcast guesting
- Find: search "community banking podcast" on Apple Podcasts/Spotify; shortlist shows
  with vendor guests (show notes = link)
- Pitch (short): founder bio + "I can walk through why 1 in 116 mortgage applications
  contains fraud and why the VOE phone call is the attack surface — with the data."
