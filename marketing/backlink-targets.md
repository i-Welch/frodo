# Backlink Target Plan

Compiled 2026-07-05. Baseline: authority score 0, 21 referring domains (all spam except submit.loans).
Goal: first 20-30 legitimate referring domains. Ordered by effort-to-impact.

## Tier 1: Free software directories (do first, ~1 day of profile writing)

Evidence: Truv (comparable verification vendor) gets links from Crunchbase, ZoomInfo, Builtin, G2, PitchBook, Glassdoor. Industry data: tier-1 listings median 312 referred sessions in first 90 days.

| Target | Cost | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Crunchbase | Free | Create company profile |
| G2 | Free tier | Create product profile under Financial Services / Verification |
| Capterra (+ GetApp, Software Advice — same Gartner network) | Free tier | One submission covers all three |
| ZoomInfo | Free (claim) | Claim company listing |
| Builtin | Free tier | Company profile |
| PitchBook | Free (claim) | Submit company data |
| Clutch / GoodFirms | Free | B2B service profiles |
| fintechvendors.com | Free | Curated fintech vendor directory |
| Product Hunt | Free | Launch post (also distribution) |
| SaaSHub / AlternativeTo | Free | List as alternative to Blend/Truv/Plaid |

## Tier 2: State banking associations (highest relevance per dollar)

These directories are browsed by the exact buyers, and .org association links carry weight. Start with SC (22 of 24 audit targets), then GA/NC.

| Target | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Independent Banks of South Carolina (myibsc.org) | Call for pricing | ICBA state affiliate; community-bank-only membership base |
| South Carolina Bankers Association (scbankers.org) | Call: 803-779-0850 | ~150 associate members, public directory page |
| Community Bankers Association of Georgia (cbaofga.com) | Call | Has a dedicated "Fintech" associate category in its directory |
| North Carolina Bankers Association (ncbankers.org) | $1,500-$3,000/yr | Affiliate directory; call (919) 781-7979 |
| ICBA corporate membership (icba.org) | $6,000/yr | National solutions directory (5 categories) + quarterly member-bank prospect list + PSP eligibility. Expensive but doubles as a sales asset |

## Tier 3: Contributed articles to trade press (free, uses existing content)

Non-promotional bylined articles with an author/company identification (= link). We already have the research; these are edits, not new writing.

| Target | Requirements | Pitch from existing content |
|---|---|---|
| ABA Banking Journal (bankingjournal.aba.com) | 500-2,000 words, educational, non-promotional, byline + company ID | 1-in-116 fraud piece, stripped of product mentions |
| BAI / ProSight Banking Strategies (bai.org) | Submission guidelines on site | "The fraud fix and the speed fix are the same fix" |
| Independent Banker (ICBA) | Editorial pitch | Fictitious-employer / VOE attack surface angle |
| Bank Director (bankdirector.com) | Contributed content program | Digital-retreat / account-opening fraud angle |
| MortgageNewsDaily | Truv has 51 links from here | Data-driven fraud or closing-time piece |
| The Financial Brand | Contributed insights | Neobank vs. community bank data |

## Tier 4: Press + local angles (ongoing)

- **PR wire for launches** (prnewswire/businesswire): one release ($350-$800) when there's real news; both wires show up in Truv's link profile
- **SC business press** (SC Biz News: Columbia Regional Business Report, Charleston Regional Business Journal, GSA Business Report): pitch the bank deep-dive data as local business stories
- **CBANC**: vendor presence where community bankers ask peers for solutions (already monitored via /cbanc-monitor)
- **Podcast guesting** (community banking podcasts; Buzzsprout/Podbean show notes link): pitch the fraud data story

## Long-term / aspirational

- Fannie Mae / Freddie Mac technology provider lists (Day 1 Certainty / AIM verified-vendor status) — Truv gets links from both GSE domains; requires actual GSE integration approval
- HousingWire / American Banker earned coverage — needs a news hook (funding, notable customer, original research report)

## Sequencing

3. Week 2-3: adapt the 1-in-116 article into a non-promotional ABA Banking Journal submission
