  2. Add your phone number (marked (XXX) XXX-XXXX).
  3. The QR code loads from api.qrserver.com — requires internet access to display. For offline print, download it at:
    https://api.qrserver.com/v1/create-qr-code/?size=400x400&data=https%3A%2F%2Freportraven.tech%2Faudit%2Fsouthern-bank-trust

  1. File → Print · Paper: Letter · Orientation: Landscape · Margins: None · Background graphics: On.
  2. Print Side A first.
  3. Flip paper on the long edge (left stays left). Print Side B.
  4. Fold in half. Cover faces out on the right.
